Startup Diagnosis

Branding

1. Do you have access to branding services for your business?



2. On a scale of 1-5 how well is your brand recognized by your target customers?(1= Not known, 5 = Well known)

3. How would you rate your brand visibility compared to the visibility of your competitors? (1 = Not visible, 2= Leading brand)

4. On a scale of 1-5, how elaborate and effective is your branding strategy?* (1 = Poor, 2= Excellent)

Finance & Accounting

1. Do you have access to finance and accounting services? (1 = Yes, 2= No)

2. On a scale of 1-5, how skilled is the person in charge of your book-keeping, financial reports generation and tax computations and filling with the relevant authorities? (1 = Poor, 5= Excellent)

3. On a scale of 1 - 5, how effective is the accounting system you are using in yourstartup to record & track transactions, as well as generate financialreports for internal decision-making, as well as for reporting to externalauthorities such as KRA, investors, and other partners? (1 = Poor, 5= Excellent)

4.On a scale of 1 - 5, how well are your financial records kept and financial reports generated in accordance with the finance and accounting standards for internal use and for external stakeholders such as KRA, investors, and other partners? (1 = Poorly kept, 5= Well kept)

Legal

1.Do you have access to legal services for your business? (1 = Yes, 2= No)

2.On a scale of 1 - 5, to what extent are legal documents and contracts drafted to minimize legal risks in your business? (1 = Never, 5= Always)

3.On a scale of 1 - 5, how well do your business operations and practices comply with relevant laws and regulations? (1 = Poorly, 5= Fully compliant)

4.On a scale of 1-5, are there provisions in place to address potential disputes and protect the interest of your business? (1 = None, 5= Wholly present)

Tech Development

1. Do you have access to Tech Services for your business? (1 = No, 2= Yes)

2.On a scale of 1-5, how easy is it for your business to adapt to technological changes and trends? (1 = Difficult, 5= Very easy)

3.On a scale of 1-5, how scalable are the technological services in your business to accommodate your growth needs? (1 = Not scalable, 5= Scalable)

4.On a scale of 1-5, how well does the technology service integrate with existing systems and software used by your business?* (1 = Poorly, 5= Fully integrated)
